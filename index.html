WOTC Tax Credits

a better way to administer

The Work Opportunity Tax Credit (WOTC) is a federal tax credit available to employers' hiring individuals who consistently face significant barriers to employment. WOTC was created to benefit job seekers and employers. While it is a federal program created by the IRS, it is administered at the state level by the Department of Workforce Development learn more.
